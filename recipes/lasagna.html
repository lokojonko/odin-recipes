Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a creamy dish with ground meat and tomatoes. One of the main ingredients aswell is pasta.

If you would like to know how to cook this dish, follow the steps below.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Lasagna pasta
  • Cream
  • Ground meat

Steps

  1. Cook the ground meat.
  2. Make a roux.
  3. Make tomato sauce.
  4. Layer the sauce and pasta.
  5. Put the dish in the oven.
